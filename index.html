The concept is simple: yellow T-shirts, instruments of all kinds, and above all, huge smiles. We bring nothing. Or rather, joy. Our tool is cheap: music. Music is unbeatable when it comes to language differences. Music is unbeatable when it comes to awakening deep-seated smiles. Music is the perfect alibi to start a conversation without embarrassment: "What's your favorite music?" In short, we're not here to provide food or clothing, but to do what's right for everyone: sing, dance, and bring a little joy to the loneliness of the homeless
